ABOUT US

Welcome to NIPOST, the leading postal service provider in Nigeria. With a history spanning over 150 years, NIPOST has been dedicated to connecting people and communities through reliable and efficient postal services. Our mission is to facilitate communication, commerce, and social interaction across the nation. NIPOST offers a wide range of services, including mail delivery, postal banking, and courier services. We are proud to be a trusted partner for individuals, businesses, and government agencies, delivering letters, packages, and important documents with care and speed. With our extensive network of post offices and dedicated staff, we are committed to providing excellent customer service and meeting the evolving needs of our customers in the digital age. Join us on this journey as we continue to innovate and transform the postal industry in Nigeria.

We have a rich history that dates back to the 19th century, and we have continually evolved to meet the changing needs of our customers. Our goal is to provide affordable, reliable, and efficient postal services nationwide and worldwide, ensuring the timely delivery of mail and parcels while exceeding customer expectations.
